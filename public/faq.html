What is clear protein, and how is it different from traditional protein powders?

Clear whey protein isolate is a type of whey protein that undergoes a unique filtration process, resulting in a clear and refreshing drink that's gut-friendly and light on the stomach. Unlike traditional protein powders, Dad Gains is clear, juicy and refreshing, making it an ideal choice for those who prefer a lighter alternative to thick, creamy protein shakes.

What's the best way to prepare Dad Gains?

Whatever is easiest for you—you have a million things to do. But if you want our opinion…

For best results, pour 12–16 oz of water in a shaker bottle. Add a scoop/packet of Dad Gains protein and shake (don't stir!) until fully dissolved. You might need to do a couple shake/let stand, shake/let stand reps to really break up the powder. Let the foam settle and pour over ice for an extra refreshing experience, and enjoy anytime of day.

If you don't want to work too hard, shake a serving as described above and let it sit in your fridge for 15 mins or more. Dad Gains goes completely clear the longer it sits, so it can be made ahead!
