Last updated: March 2026

Look, we get it. Sometimes a protein powder just isn't for you. Maybe you ordered the wrong flavor. Maybe your taste buds decided to stage a rebellion. Life happens.

Our Policy

We want you to love Dad Gains. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we'll work with you to make it right. Contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll figure out a solution—whether that's a refund, exchange, or something else.

How to Start a Return

Email us at info@dailystandardprotein.com with your order number and what went wrong. We'll get back to you within 2 business days. No runaround. No fine print games.

Refund Timeline

Once we receive your return (if applicable) and approve your refund, we'll process it within 5–7 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method.

Questions?

Email info@dailystandardprotein.com. We’re a small team—we’d rather sort it out in plain English than hide behind jargon.
