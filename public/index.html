Our Story

We built this because we’re in it too.

We’re Steven and Sebastian. We met in college, back when meal prep was a personality trait and the gym was just… what Tuesday was for.

Then kids showed up—and suddenly “eating healthy” turned into a nice idea that rarely survives contact with real life. You’re trying to feel like you’ve got energy for the day, not spend mental energy feeling bad that you didn’t make it to the gym.

So we wanted to build something for us: for the days we don’t have time to eat perfect, but we still want to feel fueled, functional, and not gross after we drink it.

Everything on the shelf felt like it was trying too hard—either loud gym-bro energy, a science fair of ingredients, or a shake that tastes like punishment. We wanted clear, simple, actually drinkable. Lemonade-first. Dad logistics included.

If you’re nodding, you’re the whole reason this exists.

— Steven & Sebastian
