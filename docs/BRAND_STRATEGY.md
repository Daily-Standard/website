## 7. Product — V1

**Format:** Clear whey protein isolate (juice-like, not a thick shake)

| Attribute | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| Protein per serving | 20g |
| Calories per serving | ~80 |
| Fat / Carbs / Sugar | 0g / 0g / 0g |
| Ingredients | 5 max (grass-fed whey isolate, citric acid, natural flavor, stevia, natural color) |
| V1 Flavor | Lightly Sweetened Vanilla |
| Packaging | Resealable stand-up pouch, 18-20 servings |

**Key attributes:**
- Grass-fed whey protein isolate — triple-filtered, lactose-free
- No artificial sweeteners — ever
- No gums — ever
- Mixes clear like juice — not a thick shake
- Third-party tested (COA per batch)
- Transparent sourcing
- Designed for daily use, not just gym days
- No creatine in V1 (reduces friction; creatine becomes product #2 once trust is built)

**Competitive reference:** Modeled after Gramms clear protein format. Best bang for your buck on protein-to-calorie ratio.
