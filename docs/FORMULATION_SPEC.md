# Daily Standard — Product Formulation Spec (V1)

**Product:** Clear Whey Protein Isolate  
**Category:** Daily Strength Nutrition  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Status:** Final spec for vendor outreach

---

## Product Overview

A clear, juice-like whey protein isolate designed for daily use. Not a thick shake. Optimized for the highest protein-to-calorie ratio with zero junk. Modeled after the Gramms clear protein format.

---

## Ingredients (Target — 5 Ingredients Max)

| # | Ingredient | Purpose | Notes |
|---|-----------|---------|-------|
| 1 | **Grass-fed whey protein isolate** | Primary protein source | Triple-filtered to remove lactose, fat, carbs. Sourced from pasture-raised, hormone-free, non-GMO cows. European or US dairy preferred. |
| 2 | **Citric acid** | Tartness / flavor balance | Standard in clear protein formulations |
| 3 | **Natural flavor** | Taste | Must be natural — no artificial flavors |
| 4 | **Stevia extract (Reb A or Reb M)** | Sweetener | Zero-calorie, natural. Light sweetness — adult palate, not candy. Reb M preferred (less bitter aftertaste). |
| 5 | **Natural color** | Visual appeal | Turmeric extract or fruit/vegetable juice powder depending on flavor |

---

## Excluded Ingredients (Non-Negotiable)

- No artificial sweeteners (sucralose, aspartame, acesulfame-K)
- No gums (xanthan, guar, gellan, carrageenan)
- No artificial flavors
- No artificial colors or dyes
- No fillers or bulking agents
- No soy lecithin
- No maltodextrin
- No sugar alcohols (erythritol, sorbitol)

## Quality & Testing Requirements

- **Source certification:** Grass-fed, hormone-free, non-GMO
- **Manufacturing:** cGMP-certified, FDA-registered facility in USA
- **Third-party lab testing:** Certificate of Analysis (COA) for each batch
  - Protein content verification (amino acid profile)
  - Heavy metals (lead, arsenic, cadmium, mercury)
  - Microbiological testing (E. coli, salmonella, yeast, mold)
  - Label accuracy verification
- **Allergen declaration:** Contains Milk
- **Shelf life target:** 18-24 months

## Notes for Co-Packer Discussion

1. We want the simplest possible formulation — 5 ingredients max
2. Taste is critical. Light sweetness, not candy. Must taste good in plain water.
3. Must dissolve clear — no cloudiness, no clumps
4. Stevia bitterness must be managed (Reb M preferred over Reb A)
5. We are open to R&D collaboration on flavor development
6. We need COA per batch — either provided by co-packer or we source independently
7. Packaging: we will provide label artwork; co-packer handles fill + seal
