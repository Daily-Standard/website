Steve is our customer. Every decision runs through him.

- Hardworking dad, has a good job, loves his kids
- Works out 2–3 times a week, but used to work out 6 times a week
- Not looking for perfect — he wants to take care of himself
- Watches trending shows on Netflix, watches trash with his wife, enjoys sports

- Skeptical of flashy supplements. Willing to pay for quality and trust.

- Does NOT want: gym bro branding, candy-sweet shakes, artificial sweeteners, complicated stacks, "mass gain" vibes

## 2. Logo

**Primary mark:** "Daily Standard" wordmark  
**Font:** DM Sans Bold, tracked at -0.03em  
**No icon/symbol for V1** — the name carries the brand. An icon or monogram (DS) can be explored later.

**Logo usage:**
- Always use the full wordmark "Daily Standard"
- Minimum clear space: height of the "D" on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, add effects, or change colors outside the palette

**Color variations:**
- Dark on light: #2C2C2C on #F5F2ED
- Light on dark: #F5F2ED on #2C2C2C
- Light on olive: #F5F2ED on #4A6741

### Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Cream** | `#F5F2ED` | 245, 242, 237 | Primary background, light surfaces |
| **Near Black** | `#2C2C2C` | 44, 44, 44 | Headlines, body text, primary buttons |
| **Muted Olive** | `#4A6741` | 74, 103, 65 | Accent, CTAs, highlights, section backgrounds |

### Secondary Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Warm Gray** | `#6B6560` | 107, 101, 96 | Subheadings, supporting text |
| **Light Warm** | `#E8E4DC` | 232, 228, 220 | Cards, dividers, subtle backgrounds |
| **Stone** | `#A8A29E` | 168, 162, 158 | Captions, placeholders, muted text |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Cards, input fields |

### Color Ratios

- **70%** Cream (#F5F2ED) — dominant background
- **20%** Near Black (#2C2C2C) — text and structure
- **10%** Olive (#4A6741) — accent and emphasis

### What NOT to Use

- No neon or bright colors
- No red, orange, or "energy" colors
- No gradients
- No black (#000000) — always use Near Black (#2C2C2C)

## 5. Tone of Voice

### We Are

- **Irreverent** — we don't take ourselves too seriously
- **Funny** — but never forced. If a joke doesn't land, cut it.
- **Honest** — we say what we mean. No marketing fluff.
- **Grounded** — masculine without being aggressive. Calm without being soft.
- **Clearly uncool... in a cool way** — we're dads. We own it.
- **TASTE matters** — if it doesn't taste good, nothing else matters. Light, clean, drinkable every day.
- **If it feels like we are trying, we stop and try again.**

### We Are NOT

- Gym bro ("crush it," "beast mode," "gains")
- Preachy ("you should be doing this")
- Clinical ("scientifically formulated for optimal bioavailability")
- Try-hard (if it feels like effort, start over)
- Corporate ("we leverage synergies to deliver value")

### Voice Test

Before publishing any copy, ask: **Would Steve say this to a friend at a BBQ?**

- If yes: ship it.
- If it sounds like a billboard: rewrite it.
- If it sounds like a gym poster: delete it.

### Example Copy

| Instead of... | We say... |
|---------------|-----------|
| "Maximize your protein intake for optimal performance" | "Just get your protein in." |
| "Scientifically formulated grass-fed whey isolate" | "5 ingredients. All of them real." |
| "Fuel your gains" | "Fuel the man, not the ego." |
| "Join our community of elite athletes" | "For dads who give a damn." |
| "LIMITED TIME OFFER!!!" | "Be first. Get it for less." |

## 6. Product Positioning

### What Daily Standard IS

- **Clear protein** — mixes like juice, not a thick shake
- Grass-fed whey protein isolate, triple-filtered, lactose-free
- 20g protein, ~80 calories, 0g sugar per serving
- 5 ingredients max — no exceptions
- No artificial sweeteners, no gums, no fillers
- Adult palate — light, clean sweetness. Not candy.
- Designed for **daily use** — not just gym days

### What Daily Standard is NOT

- Not a mass gainer
- Not a meal replacement
- Not a thick, milky shake
- Not a pre-workout or energy supplement
- Not for bodybuilders — it's for dads who want to take care of themselves

### Emotional Tone

Masculine grounded. Calm premium. Long-term focused.  
Not aggressive. Not soft. Not jokey.

## 7. Photography & Imagery

### Style

- **Natural light** — morning light, golden hour, kitchen settings
- **Real environments** — kitchen counter, car cup holder, desk, backyard
- **Not staged gym shots** — no one is flexing
- **People:** Real dads, real bodies, candid moments. Not models.
- **Product:** Clean, minimal, on a simple surface. Let the packaging speak.

### Never

- Stock photos of muscular men
- Gym interiors or weight rooms
- Shaker bottles dripping with sweat
- "Before and after" body shots
- Neon or high-contrast sports lighting

## 8. Packaging Design Direction

### Pouch Design

- **Material:** Matte-finish stand-up resealable pouch
- **Primary color:** Cream (#F5F2ED) or Near Black (#2C2C2C)
- **Layout:** Clean, generous whitespace, minimal design
- **Front panel:** Brand name, flavor, protein amount (20g), one-line differentiator
- **Back panel:** Supplement Facts, ingredient list, brand story (2-3 sentences), allergen info, barcode

## 9. Digital Design

### Website / Landing Page

- Background: Cream (#F5F2ED)
- Cards: White (#FFFFFF) with subtle shadow
- Buttons: Near Black (#2C2C2C) with Cream text; Olive (#4A6741) on hover
- Forms: White inputs with warm gray (#D5D0C8) borders
- Navigation: Fixed, blurred background, minimal

### Email

- Clean, text-forward design
- Cream background, dark text
- Single CTA button per email
- Tone: like a note from a friend, not a marketing blast

### Social Media

- Muted, natural tones
- Text-on-image posts: use Cream on Near Black or Near Black on Cream
- Video: natural settings, founder-led, phone-quality is fine (authentic > polished)

When creating any brand asset, verify:

- [ ] Logo has proper clear space
- [ ] Passes the "are we trying too hard?" test
